Manifest & Grouping

If you are planning on shipping over 50 shipments per day then you should opt to include them in a Manifest. Failure to comply may result in shipments being refused by Canada Post. A manifest must be given to the Canada Post representative at pickup. If you're unfamiliar with manifests, visit our video tutorials section.

Continuous Inbound Freight

If you have shipments that originate outside of Canada but are being delivered directly to a Canada Post plant, use this element to identify these shipments as continuous inbound freight (CIF). This option allows you to be eligible for Canadian sales tax exemptions. You will need to provide Canada Post with documentation that shows proof of origin, such as a Canadian customs document or bill of lading.



Expected Mailing Date

Shipments are priced using the current shipment date and are re-priced at time of transmit if transmitted at a later date. Use this field to indicate when you expect to hand the shipment to Canada Post.
